Terrain Representation
This piece was developed for a Graphic Design and Cartography Master's level course. The primary goal of the project was to practice developing a realistic terrain representation basemap, and the premise for which the map would be used was to propose a fictitious course for a bike race that traversed 2,100miles over the course of 21 days, with two rests after 7 and 14 days.

This project involved ArcGIS, Google MyMaps, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.
