Longform Infographic
This piece was developed for a Graphic Design and Cartography Master's level course. I was tasked with creating a full length longform infographic on a topic of my choice that included a bivariate map. I volunteer my time with the non-profit Oberlin Community Services, and reached out to them and asked what might be a helpful topic to tackle and the result of that collaboration is what you see below.

This project involved ArcGIS, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop
