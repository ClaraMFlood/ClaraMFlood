About







Clara Margaret is a recent honors geology graduate of Oberlin College who loves animals almost as much as she loves rocks, maps and analyzing spatial data. Her research on garnets from the Catalina subduction complex was presented at the annual Geological Society of America's 2017 conference. Currently working as a GIS instructor at a top-tier liberal arts college, she sees her GIS work as art: creating beautiful images that tell a dynamic story. Her gentle, patient spirit and thoughtful encouragement make her a natural teacher with students of all levels and make her an invaluable addition to her academic community.
